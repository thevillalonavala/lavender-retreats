Lavender Hills

A serene countryside retreat nestled among rolling hills and fragrant lavender fields. Lavender Hills offers a rare kind of quiet — the sort where you notice birdsong, slow your breathing, and truly rest.

The open-plan kitchen and living area spill onto a sun-warmed terrace, where a private pool shimmers beneath an open sky. Evenings here are unforgettable — watching the last light dissolve over the hills with a glass of local wine in hand.

Ideal for families, couples travelling together, or a group of friends seeking space, beauty, and a slower pace of life.

Property 2 — Karjat

Lavender Crest

Sleeps 15 · 4 Bedrooms

Perched at the crown of the hillside, Lavender Crest commands one of the most breathtaking panoramas in the region. This luxurious four-bedroom villa was designed for those who refuse to compromise — on space, on style, or on the view.

The wraparound terrace becomes the heart of daily life here: morning coffee as the mist lifts from the valley, alfresco lunches in dappled shade, long dinners beneath the stars.

Lavender Crest is the perfect setting for milestone occasions, extended family gatherings, or simply a week of extraordinary living shared with the people you love most.

Getting Here

Lavender Hills

Lonavala, Maharashtra

📍

A4, NG Valley, Near Buvanchi Misal, Frichley Hill Rd,
Behind Fariyas Resort Lonavala,
Frichley Hills, Tungarli, Lonavala

📍 Open in Google Maps

How to Reach

✈️

By Air: Pune International Airport is approximately 65 km away (1.5 hrs drive). Mumbai's Chhatrapati Shivaji Airport is around 95 km (2–2.5 hrs drive).

🚆

By Train: Lonavala Railway Station is the nearest railhead, just 4 km from the property. Well connected to both Mumbai and Pune with frequent express and local trains.

🚗

By Road: Take the Mumbai–Pune Expressway (NH48) and exit at Lonavala. Head towards Tungarli and follow Frichley Hill Road. Look for Fariyas Resort — the property is behind it. GPS navigation works well up to the main gate.

🚕

Local Transfers: Cabs are readily available from Lonavala station and the expressway. We recommend pre-booking for a smooth arrival.

How to Reach

✈️

By Air: Pune International Airport is approximately 60 km away (1.5 hrs drive). Mumbai's Chhatrapati Shivaji Airport is around 90 km (2–2.5 hrs drive).

🚆

By Train: Karjat Railway Station is the nearest major railhead, approximately 25 km from the property. Karjat is well connected to Mumbai and Pune with regular express and local trains.

🚗

By Road: Take the Mumbai–Pune Expressway (NH48) and exit towards Lonavala/Khandala. Head towards Jambrung village. Use the Google Maps link above for precise navigation — the final stretch involves a scenic narrow road, so drive carefully.

🚕

Local Transfers: We recommend pre-booking a cab from Lonavala or Pune. Local auto-rickshaws are available but may not reach the property directly. Please contact us for recommended transfer services.
